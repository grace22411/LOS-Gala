About The LOS Gala

Lagos in December was originally conceived as a look back at a time when everything and anything was possible. We wanted to re-imagine Lagos State during that first December of Nigeria’s Independence in 1960 and offer a romanticized experience grounded in history.

With the city as our inspiration, we have taken every day elements and curated a night of reflection and revelry. December, often a time to gather and reconnect takes the normal hustle and bustle to a fever pitch.

With this presentation, we take a moment to stop and reflect on how far we have come, pay respect to those that paved the way, and celebrate the things we tend to overlook- the things that make Lagos unique and special.

Welcome to The LOS Gala: Lagos In December, a night when everything and anything is possible.
